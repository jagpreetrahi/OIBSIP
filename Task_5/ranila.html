Rani Lakshmibai

1828-1858

The Warrior Queen

Rani Lakshmibai, the Queen of Jhansi, was one of the leading figures in the 1857 Rebellion against British rule. She displayed extraordinary courage in battle and died fighting for India's freedom.

Contributions:
  • Led the 1857 revolt in Jhansi.
  • Fought bravely against British forces.
  • Symbolized resistance and bravery.
