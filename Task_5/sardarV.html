Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel

1875-1950

The Iron Man of India

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel played a crucial role in India's integration. He united more than 500 princely states to form a single nation after independence.

Contributions:
  • Integrated princely states into India.
  • First Deputy Prime Minister and Home Minister.
  • Promoted unity and nationalism.
