Bhagat Singh

1907-1931

The Revolutionary Hero

Bhagat Singh was one of India's greatest revolutionaries, known for his role in the freedom struggle. He was a member of the Hindustan Socialist Republican Association (HSRA) and is remembered for his courageous acts, including the Assembly bomb incident and the assassination of British officer J.P. Saunders.

He was executed on March 23, 1931, at the age of 23, becoming a martyr for India's independence.
